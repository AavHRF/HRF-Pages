Ministerial Bios

Below are bios of all our government members

Matthew Karimi

Matthew is the Kanzler, or Chancellor, of Hartfelden. He is apart of Hartfelden's military, serving as an officer of the rank of Rittmeister. Before dedicating his time to Hartfelden, he was involved in multiple other regional governments in unofficial and official capacities.

Aav von Hartfelden

Aav Ritter von Hartfelden is the Minister of Foreign Affairs for Hartfelden. During their tenure as Minister, they have overseen the development of foreign relations with multiple regions, and have secured treaties. They are also an active participant in the legislative process of Hartfelden, holding the position of Praesident of the Reichstag, the lower legislative house of Hartfelden.

Malachor Ren


Malachor Ren is the Minister of Integration for Hartfelden. During his time as Minister, he has overseen the welcoming and integration of many newcomers to Hartfelden, several becoming citizens. Ren is also an Inquisitor of Hartfelden, working to enforce the region's rules and keep it safe.

Bobberino

Bobberino is an NS veteran of roughly two years. He is most known for his work in The North Pacific and The Rejected Realms, and through his connections to Matthew and Scott he became involved in Hartfelden and was brought into the community. In Hartfelden he currently sits as Domestic Minister, in charge of cultural affairs, assisting in outreach, and on a more personal level, offering an experienced presence in Matthew's cabinet.
